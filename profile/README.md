## 📖 About Us

At **Cerify.ai**, we believe that smart contract security shouldn't be a bottleneck for Web3 innovation. Traditional audits are slow, costly, and often incomplete—leaving projects exposed and institutions hesitant to adopt decentralized technologies.

We are building a smarter, scalable monitoring system that transforms smart contract verification into a streamlined, automated process without sacrificing rigor. Born from research at IIT Delhi and IIM Bangalore, Cerify bridges the gap between the mathematical certainty of formal verification and the rapid adaptability of artificial intelligence.

## 🧠 Our Engine: Neuro-Symbolic AI

We don't just rely on standard LLMs, which can generate probabilistic and hallucinated outputs. Security demands 100% deterministic, reason-governed results. 

Our unique **Neuro-Symbolic AI architecture** integrates three core layers:
1. **Parsing & Intermediate Representation:** Converting Solidity, Vyper, Kotlin, and other languages into a structured format for deep analysis.
2. **Symbolic Execution:** Exploring all possible program states and execution paths to verify security properties against defined constraints.
3. **AI-Powered Fault Analysis:** Prioritizing issues based on exploitability, learning from historical vulnerabilities to reduce false positives, and providing actionable remediation pathways.

## ⚡ What We Deliver

- **Contextual Auditing:** We don't just scan for known bugs; we analyze how your unique business logic and architecture can be exploited.
- **Deep Vulnerability Detection:** Identifying threats across 14+ critical security classes, including Reentrancy, Integer Overflow, and Unchecked External Calls.
- **Fault Localization:** Pinpointing the exact root cause of failures with precise execution trace analysis.
- **Unmatched Speed:** Cutting audit times from weeks down to a single day, empowering builders to deploy with confidence faster than ever.

## 🌐 Ecosystem Impact

Whether you are a startup building the next DeFi protocol or an enterprise scaling Web3 infrastructure, our mission is to provide affordable, developer-friendly security tooling that scales with your ambition.
