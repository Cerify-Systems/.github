# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in any of our repositories or products, please report it responsibly.

**Do not open a public issue for security vulnerabilities.**

Instead, please email us directly at [support@cerify.ai](mailto:support@cerify.ai) with:

- A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Any relevant logs, screenshots, or proof-of-concept code

## What to Expect

- We will acknowledge receipt of your report as soon as possible.
- We will investigate and keep you updated on our progress.
- Once resolved, we will coordinate with you on responsible disclosure timing, if applicable.

## Scope

This policy applies to all repositories and services owned by this organization. Please do not test for vulnerabilities against production systems without prior authorization — contact us first to arrange a safe testing environment if needed.

Thank you for helping keep our users and systems secure.
