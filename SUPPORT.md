# Support

Thanks for your interest in Cerify.ai! Here's how to get help depending on what you need.

## For contributors

- **Questions about contributing, our workflow, or an open issue** — open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/orgs/Cerify-Systems/projects/3) in the relevant repository, or comment on the existing issue you're working from.
- **Found a bug?** Check the project board first to see if it's already tracked, then open a new issue if not, following the steps in [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md).

## For security concerns

Please do not open a public issue. See [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for how to report vulnerabilities responsibly.

## For everything else

- **General inquiries, partnerships, or product questions** — reach us at [support@cerify.ai](mailto:support@cerify.ai)
- **Learn more about what we do** — visit [cerify.ai](https://cerify.ai)
